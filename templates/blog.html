Professional Watermarking: Protect Your Images

Published on January 8, 2025 Brand Protection

Watermarking is essential for photographers, designers, and content creators. Our watermark tool lets you add text or logo watermarks with transparency control, positioning, and batch processing capabilities.

Why Watermark Your Images?
Copyright Protection: Establish ownership and prevent unauthorized use of your creative work.
Brand Promotion: Increase visibility and drive traffic back to your website or portfolio.
Watermarking Best Practices
  • Positioning: Bottom-right corner for subtle branding, center for maximum protection
  • Transparency: 30-50% opacity maintains image quality while ensuring visibility
  • Size: 10-15% of image width prevents easy removal while staying professional
  • Font Choice: Use clean, readable fonts for text watermarks
